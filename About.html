Who are we?

Interact Ripensis Timișoara is a youth volunteer club (ages 12–18), part of Rotary International, dedicated to community service, personal development, and promoting values of friendship and social responsibility among young people from Timișoara.

What we do?

We organize events, charity projects, fundraising campaigns, volunteer actions, and promote educational and impactful community initiatives.We belong to the global Interact network – young people involved in Rotary actions, cultural exchanges, and value promotion.

Why we do?

We all share the same objective: making the world a better place. By our common sense , we work together in making different ideas a life changing move for people in need. We work as voluntiers not employes , so everything we do is by heart not for money.

More about us.

Our group is formed by over 70 active members who take part in all the activities that are organized.

We have regular meetings to discuss about the upcoming or ongoing projects or to vote new projects to organize in the future.

We also recruit new memebers twice a year , once in March and once in September.
